National

National Youth Achievement Award 2024

The National Youth Achievement Award is Singapore's premier youth development programme, modelled after the Duke of Edinburgh's International Award and launched in 1992. It is self-directed and non-competitive, requiring participants to set and pursue personal goals across four sections: Community Service, Skills, Physical Recreation, and an Adventurous Journey. Earning the Silver Award required a minimum of 12 months of sustained commitment, with set hours of regular activity logged in each section and verified by instructors, before submitting a full Award Record Book and reflective diary for assessment.

🥈 Silver Award
